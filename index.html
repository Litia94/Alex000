Ender Alex

Unimplemented Minecraft Entity / ???
Danger Level: ★★★★★

Overview

Ender Alex is an unofficial and unimplemented entity rumored to be born from a catastrophic fusion of deleted Alex test data and Enderman source code. She is described not as a mob, but as a presence summoned from the world's side.

Reported Versions

  • Java Edition 1.12.2
  • Java Edition 1.8.9
  • Java Edition 1.16.5 (Rare)
  • Bedrock Edition (Imperfect replication)

Reports are concentrated in legacy or transitional versions between major updates.

History of Discovery

First rumors appeared around 2014–2015, originating from a now-deleted overseas forum. A user claimed that entering /spawn Ender Alex caused an immediate world crash. By 2016, the rumor spread to Japan with altered ritual conditions.

Summoning Conditions (Rumored)

  • Create a cross-shaped grave using Obsidian
  • Place a sign reading "Foolish creature"
  • Ignite Netherrack at the top and bottom
  • Slay multiple Endermen nearby
  • Enter: /spawn Ender Alex

Abnormalities After Re-logging

  • Terrain converts into End Stone
  • Torches disappear
  • Chest contents replaced with Ender Pearls
  • Signs change text to "Still here?"

Join Message

After sufficient time:

"Ender_Alex joined the game"

She does not appear on the Tab list.

Sightings and Behavior

  • Visible only for a split second when turning around
  • Unknown and unused sounds play
  • Villages found burning

Appearance

  • Base body identical to Alex
  • Left side resembles an Enderman
  • Both eyes glow purple
  • Purple particles constantly emitted

Evidence Image

Final Encounter

She does not attack immediately. Once conditions are met, the following appears:

"Foolish creature. Now you understand."

She then teleports behind the player and kills them instantly. Upon respawn, the world name may change to:

[WORLD DATA CORRUPTED]

This entity does not officially exist.
